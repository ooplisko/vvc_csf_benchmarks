Image benchmark binaries
========================

vvenc_default.exe
  Upstream/default VVenC encoder. It does not know --CSFScalingList.

vvenc_csf.exe
  CSF-enabled VVenC encoder. Use --CSFScalingList 1 for the custom matrix.

vvenc_csf_trace.exe
  CSF encoder built with ENABLE_TRACING=1. Use it only when generating
  partition maps with --tracefile and --tracerule.

vvenc_default_trace.exe
  Upstream/default encoder built with ENABLE_TRACING=1. Use it only when
  generating baseline partition maps with --tracefile and --tracerule.

vvdecapp.exe
  VVdeC decoder used to check that produced bitstreams decode.

Example
-------

.\vvenc_default.exe `
  --InputFile input.yuv `
  --SourceWidth 512 `
  --SourceHeight 512 `
  --FrameRate 1 `
  --FramesToBeEncoded 1 `
  --QP 32 `
  --preset medium `
  --BitstreamFile baseline.vvc `
  --ReconFile baseline_rec.yuv

.\vvenc_csf.exe `
  --InputFile input.yuv `
  --SourceWidth 512 `
  --SourceHeight 512 `
  --FrameRate 1 `
  --FramesToBeEncoded 1 `
  --QP 32 `
  --preset medium `
  --CSFScalingList 1 `
  --BitstreamFile csf.vvc `
  --ReconFile csf_rec.yuv

.\vvdecapp.exe -b csf.vvc -o csf_dec.yuv
